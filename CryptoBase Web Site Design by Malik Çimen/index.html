    • Predicting Bitcoin's price changes is a dangerous sport of probability - no one is always right. Investors trading in traditional fiat money markets use Bitcoin price charts to match competing cryptocurrencies and their prevailing exchange rates. These charts provide important data about the relationship of one currency to another and the corresponding value of each currency. The two main ways of predicting price improvement are defined as fundamental analysis and technical analysis. Fundamental analysis explores the underlying strengths of a market, a firm, or a security, while technical analysis tries to determine the course of prices based on previous market data, especially traditional prices and volumes found in bitcoin price charts.
